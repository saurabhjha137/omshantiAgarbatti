About Us

Om Shanti Agarbatti is a new and innovative incense stick company that was established in 2022. We are passionate young entrepreneurs committed to providing our customers with the best quality and authentic products.
With a large variety of incense sticks to choose from, we have already sold over 1000 packets in a short time, catering to the needs of everyone. We are a local business, proud of our community and committed to delivering the best quality at a fair price. Our passion for excellence and dedication to customer satisfaction make us the go-to choice for anyone looking for pure and authentic incense sticks.
