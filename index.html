About Us

Om Shanti Agarbatti is a new and innovative incense stick company that was established in 2022. We are passionate young entrepreneurs committed to providing our customers with the best quality and authentic products.
With a large variety of incense sticks to choose from, we have already sold over 1000 packets in a short time, catering to the needs of everyone.
